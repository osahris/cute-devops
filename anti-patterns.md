A field guide to industry patterns that aren't *cute*: practices that
look reasonable on the surface but produce friction, opaque systems,
or unhappy operators in the long run. Each entry names what it is,
why it's tempting, and what to do instead — usually a pointer over
to the [Patterns!](./patterns.md) library.
